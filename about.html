Our Mission

Our Approach

We recognize that finance and operations professionals face unique challenges: limited time for training, pressure to deliver immediate results, and the need to learn technologies that integrate seamlessly with existing workflows. Our curriculum reflects these realities.

The Meta Tech Program

Meta Tech represents the culmination of IFS expertise in finance technology education. The program integrates seven critical competency areas into a comprehensive curriculum that prepares professionals for the full spectrum of modern analytical work.

Participants learn to extract and transform data with Power Query, perform advanced analysis in Excel, build sophisticated data models using DAX, create compelling visualizations in Power BI, deploy enterprise solutions through Power BI Services, and automate workflows with n8n and Typebot.

This integrated approach ensures that graduates understand not just individual tools but the complete analytics ecosystem‚Äîpositioning them to deliver transformative value in finance, operations, and strategic planning roles.
