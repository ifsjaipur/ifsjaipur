What is Meta Tech?

A comprehensive, industry-aligned course designed for professionals who need to harness data for competitive advantage

Meta Tech is an integrated professional development program that bridges the gap between traditional finance and operations roles and the data-driven demands of modern business. Rather than treating Excel, Power BI, and automation as separate tools, Meta Tech teaches them as a unified ecosystem designed to solve real-world business challenges.

The course is built around a practical workflow: extracting and transforming data with Power Query, analyzing it with advanced Excel techniques, building robust data models using DAX, creating insightful visualizations in Power BI, deploying enterprise solutions through Power BI Services, and automating repetitive processes with n8n and Typebot.

This is not a collection of isolated software tutorials. Meta Tech equips professionals with an integrated skillset that reflects how data analytics actually works in finance departments, operations teams, and executive offices. Participants learn to think analytically, automate intelligently, and communicate findings effectively‚Äîcapabilities that define high-performing professionals in today's business environment.
