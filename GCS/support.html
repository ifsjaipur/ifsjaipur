Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. We are committed to processing all data deletion requests promptly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

What Gets Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Your account activity and usage data
  • Any content or materials you've created within the App
  • All associated metadata and records

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Revoke Access Through Google

  1. Go to your Google Account Permissions page
  2. Find our App in the list of third-party apps
  3. Click "Remove Access"
  4. Send us an email at info@ifsjaipur.com to request complete data deletion

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to: info@ifsjaipur.com
  2. Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include: The email address associated with your account
  4. We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
  5. Data deletion will be completed within 30 days

Important Notes:

  • Irreversible: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Backup copies: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted
  • Legal retention: Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and security, we may need to verify your identity before processing a data deletion request. This may involve confirming your email address or answering security questions.

Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 48 hours of receiving your request
  • Verification: 1-3 business days (if required)
  • Deletion: Within 30 days of verification
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email when the process is complete

Download Your Data

Before requesting deletion, you may want to download a copy of your data. To request a data export:

  1. Email us at info@ifsjaipur.com
  2. Subject line: "Data Export Request"
  3. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reactivate my account after deletion?

No, once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You would need to create a new account by signing in again with Google.

Will deleting my data affect other users?

Your personal information will be deleted, but shared educational content may remain accessible to other users unless specifically requested otherwise.

How do I know my data has been deleted?

You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion process is complete. You can also verify by attempting to sign in - your account will no longer be recognized.

What if I only want to delete specific information?

Contact us with your specific request, and we'll work with you to accommodate your needs where possible.
